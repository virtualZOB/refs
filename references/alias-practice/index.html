    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Generic private message to the ATC position C08 advising them "aircraft not responding to comm-change".
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Rolling Call Notification private message to D1E advising from "RWY 19".
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Rejected Takeoff Notification private message to D1E.
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Missed Approach Notification private message to D1E advising heading 210 and 9,000 assigned "H210 9K".
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Down-Time Notification private message to D1W.
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Release Request private message to D1E.
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Release Approved private message to E1E advising the aircraft is released for departure at 4min from the current time.
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Point-Out request private message to G23 and give VIO as the point of reference and advise that the aircraft "will descend below your altitudes once clear of traffic"
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Handoff Notification private message to CEN with KNOOK as the reference point and give control to CEN.
    GROUND TO GROUND COMMUNICATION
    With the subject aircraft selected, send a Approval Request (APREQ) private message to 70 requesting the aircraft be allowed to proceed "direct KROC".

    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Using a ZOB alias command, send a contact-me message to the selected aircraft.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Instruct the selected aircraft to contact the ATC position D1E.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Instruct the selected aircraft to monitor the ATC position D1E.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Announce that you will be closing in 5min.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Announce that you are now closed.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    A pilot requests a frequency change from GND to TWR when they have reached their assigned departure runway. What alias command would you do to send information to this pilot informing them that they are expected to switch to TWR in this situation without prompting or authorization to do so?
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Using the ZOB alias command, private message the selected aircraft asking for their FAA equipment suffix code and provides a link to a tutorial showing them how to determine their code.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Using a ZOB alias command, private message the selected aircraft advising them that their N-number format seems to be incorrect and links them to the FAA site to help them figure out how to reconnect with a correctly formatted callsign.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-General
    Using a ZOB alias command, private message the selected aircraft advising them that their air-carrier callsign format seems to be incorrect and links them to the FAA site to help them figure out how to reconnect with a correctly formatted callsign.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Clearance Delivery
    Inform an aircraft that their readback is correct.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Clearance Delivery
    Issue a CRAFT clearance as filed with an initial altitude of 10,000. If departure is online, their ATC Position ID is D1E. End the root command with a U if departure is offline. .tCraftAf 10k D1E
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Clearance Delivery
    Issue a CRAFT clearance with a full route and an initial altitude of 10,000. If departure is online, their ATC Position ID is D1E. End the root command with a U if departure is offline.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Clearance Delivery
    Issue a CRAFT clearance with the HHOWE4, LNCON as the endpoint, and issue Climb Via SID. If departure is online, their ATC Position ID is D1E. End the root command with a U if departure is offline.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Clearance Delivery
    Issue a CRAFT clearance with the HHOWE4, LNCON as the endpoint, and issue Climb Via SID, except maintain 10k. If departure is online, their ATC Position ID is D1E. End the root command with a U if departure is offline.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Ground Control
    Issue taxi instructions to runway 18 via A, B, C and advise the aircraft to "use caution, a united B737 pushing back near B".
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Ground Control
    Issue taxi instructions to parking via A B C.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Local Control
    Clear the selected aircraft to takeoff on RWY 19L.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Local Control
    Clear the selected aircraft to land on RWY 19L.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Instruct the selected aircraft to squawk an assigned code and ident.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Advise the selected aircraft they are RADAR Contact.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Advise the selected aircraft they are RADAR Contact with a position relative to DXO.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Instruct the selected aircraft to fly heading 180.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Instruct the selected aircraft to turn left heading 180 or instruct them to turn right.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Advise the selected aircraft that their RADAR services are terminated.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Advise the selected aircraft that they are leaving your airspace and that their radar services are terminated.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-RADAR - General
    Instruct the selected aircraft to climb or descend and maintain FL180
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Departure
    Instruct the selected aircraft to hold for release.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Departure
    Issue a Release-For-Departure clearance that includes a 10min void time from the current time and an advisory to contact the your no later than 15min of intentions if not off in time.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Approach
    Issue a standard PTAC given the relative position to KPTK, heading 060 and 2.7k to fly until established and then the clearance to fly the ILS approach for RWY 9R.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Approach
    Advise the selected aircraft of their position relative to their destination and then asks them to report the field in sight.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Approach
    Clear the selected aircraft for the visual approach to runway 9R or to KPTK.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Enroute
    Issue a crossing restriction to the pilot for SLT.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Enroute
    Issue a crossing restriction to the pilot for PSB when not landing KLGA.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Enroute
    Issue a crossing restriction to the pilot for SLT when landing KLGA.
    TEXT TO PILOTS-Enroute
    Issue a crossing restriction to the pilot for CUTTA when PIT is landing West.
